C-CORE

C-CORE has been assisting Canadian SMEs for many years to identify markets for their Remotely Piloted Aircraft System (RPAS) expertise, and demonstrate application capability for prospective customers. It has also worked with the industry to address the regulatory environment necessary for broad operational RPAS implementation and sector growth.

In recent years, C-CORE has invested resources to address the data management issues facing the RPAS industry. It is continually evaluating the various generic data management solutions for their applicability for application to earth observation data management, including RPAS related data, and has also developed CoreSightTM - an internal geospatial data management solution that is targeted to specific earth observation scenarios.
